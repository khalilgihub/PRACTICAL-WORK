Characters of A Silent Voice

Back to Movie
Shoya Ishida - Miyu Irino

The protagonist who seeks redemption for bullying Shoko in elementary school.

Shoko Nishimiya - Saori Hayami

A deaf girl who transfers to Shoya's elementary school and faces bullying.

Yuzuru Nishimiya - Aoi Yūki

Shoko's younger sister who is protective of her and enjoys photography.

Miyako Ishida - Yukino Satsuki

Shoya's mother who works hard to support her family after being abandoned by her husband.

Yaeko Nishimiya - Akiko Hiramatsu

Shoko's mother who wants to protect her daughter no matter what.

Miyoko Sahara - Yui Ishikawa

Shoko's first friend in the elementary school.

Miki Kawai - Han Megumi

A popular girl in class who often avoids taking responsibility for her actions.

Naoka Ueno - Yuuki Kaneko

A childhood friend of Shoya's who remains hostile toward Shoko.

Tomohiro Nagatsuka - Kenshō Ono

Shoya's first friend in high school who is very loyal and energetic.

Satoshi Mashibas - Toshiyuki Toyonaga

Interested in being friends with Shoya.
